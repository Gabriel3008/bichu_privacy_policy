Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

Bichu is a score-tracking app for the card games Belote, Biriba, and Tichu. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Game data (stored locally): Match scores, team names, and game history are saved on your device using SQLite and shared preferences. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through your phone's backup service.
  • Usage analytics: The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as which features are used and how often the app is opened. No personally identifiable information is included in this data.

How We Use Your Information

  • Game data is used solely to display your match history and statistics within the app.
  • Analytics data is used to understand how the app is used and to improve future versions.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics, which is subject to Google's privacy policy. Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email address, or any other directly identifying information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party beyond the analytics service described above.

Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google Firebase per their standard retention policy.

Children's Privacy

Bichu is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
